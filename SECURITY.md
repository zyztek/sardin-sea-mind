## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of our fishing cooperatives very seriously. If you have found a vulnerability, please do NOT create a public issue.

### Process

1. Email us directly at **<security@sardin-ai.com>** (fictional for now).
2. We will acknowledge your email within 48 hours.
3. We will provide a timeline for the fix.
4. Once fixed, we will publicly credit you in our release notes (unless you prefer anonymity).

### Sensitive Data

Please do not include sensitive real-world data in your reports. Use the dummy data provided in the `dev-data` folder.
